Chemify

I created Chemify together with a team as an educational app that makes learning the periodic table more fun through gamification. It features interactive games, including element card battles, periodic table puzzles, and quizzes. The app also includes a flashcard-based memorization tool, allowing users to explore the detail of each element. With a user-friendly design, Chemify turns chemistry learning into a fun and interactive experience.

Users can also easily filter events based on specific sports categories, making it convenient to find their favorite sports.
